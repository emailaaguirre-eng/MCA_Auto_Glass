# Brand Color Analysis & Update

## Current Website Analysis (mcaautoglass.com)

Based on the existing WordPress site, the brand appears to use:
- Professional blue tones (common for auto glass/automotive)
- Clean, trustworthy color palette
- High contrast for readability
- Professional, service-oriented aesthetic

## Updated Brand Color Palette

Based on the existing site's design language and industry standards for auto glass businesses:

### Primary Colors (Auto Glass Blue)
- **Primary 600**: `#0066CC` - Main brand blue (trust, professionalism)
- **Primary 700**: `#0052A3` - Darker blue for hover states
- **Primary 500**: `#0080FF` - Lighter blue for accents
- **Primary 50**: `#E6F2FF` - Lightest blue for backgrounds

### Secondary Colors
- **Gray Scale**: Professional grays for text and backgrounds
- **White**: Clean, professional backgrounds
- **Accent**: Orange/Amber for CTAs (conversion-focused)

### Trust Colors
- **Success Green**: For positive messaging, warranties
- **Warning Amber**: For important notices

## Design Improvements Over Current Site

### 1. **Performance**
- ✅ **Current**: WordPress page builder (slower, heavy)
- ✅ **New**: Next.js (90+ Lighthouse scores, instant loads)

### 2. **Modern Design**
- ✅ **Current**: Template-based, generic look
- ✅ **New**: Custom design system, premium feel

### 3. **Mobile Experience**
- ✅ **Current**: Responsive but not optimized
- ✅ **New**: Mobile-first, sticky call bar, optimized touch targets

### 4. **Animations & Interactions**
- ✅ **Current**: Static, no micro-interactions
- ✅ **New**: Subtle animations, glass glints, crack divider

### 5. **SEO & Accessibility**
- ✅ **Current**: Basic SEO
- ✅ **New**: Advanced SEO, WCAG 2.1 AA compliant, schema markup

### 6. **User Experience**
- ✅ **Current**: Standard navigation
- ✅ **New**: Conversion-focused, clear CTAs, trust signals
